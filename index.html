Seed Phrase Knowledge Base

  • Word Count: Seed phrases typically consist of 12, 18, or 24 words. The most common length is 12 words.
  • Word List: The words come from a predefined list of 2048 words (BIP-39 standard), ensuring each word is unique and easily recognizable.
  • Checksum: The last word includes a checksum to verify the integrity of the phrase, helping detect errors.

Critical: Never enter your real seed phrase on any website, even if it claims to be secure. This tool is for demonstration purposes only.

  • Offline Use: For maximum security, download this page and use it offline without an internet connection.
  • Backup: Always write down your seed phrase on paper and store it in multiple secure locations.
  • No Digital Copies: Never store your seed phrase digitally (no photos, cloud storage, or text files).

When you enter a seed phrase, this tool performs several validation checks:

  1. 1. Word List Validation: Each word must be present in the BIP-39 standard word list for your selected language.
  2. 2. Checksum Verification: The tool calculates what the checksum should be based on your entered words and verifies it matches the last word in your phrase.
  3. 3. Length Check: Verifies the phrase has a valid length (12, 18, or 24 words).

Tip: A valid seed phrase will pass all checks. If any check fails, the phrase is invalid and should not be used to secure cryptocurrency funds.
